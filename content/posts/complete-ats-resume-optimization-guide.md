# Complete ATS Resume Optimization Guide

Use this pillar guide to improve resume writing strategy, pass ATS screening, and increase interview callbacks.

## What is an ATS Resume

An ATS resume is structured for machine readability while staying persuasive for human recruiters.

## How ATS Systems Scan Resumes

Applicant tracking systems parse headings, dates, employers, and keyword relevance against job requirements.

## Resume Keywords That Pass ATS

Match exact skills from target job descriptions and embed them naturally in experience bullets.

## Common Resume Mistakes

- Using graphics-heavy templates that ATS tools cannot parse.
- Omitting measurable impact.
- Missing role-specific keywords.

## ATS Resume Template

Create a simple structure with summary, core skills, professional experience, education, and certifications.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is an ATS-friendly resume? | A: It is a resume formatted and written so applicant tracking systems can parse details accurately and match role keywords.
Q: How many pages should a resume be? | A: Most professionals should target one to two pages based on experience depth and relevance.
Q: Do recruiters read resumes or only ATS? | A: ATS filters first in many workflows, but recruiters still review shortlisted resumes manually.
